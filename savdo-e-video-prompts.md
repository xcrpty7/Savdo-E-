# Savdo-E — Video Generatsiya Promptlari

Quyidagi promptlarni istalgan AI video platformasiga (HeyGen, Runway, Pika, CapCut AI, Canva) copy-paste qilib ishlatsang bo'ladi.

---

## 🎯 ASOSIY PROMPT (universal — eng ko'p platformalarda ishlaydi)

```
Create a vertical (9:16) 20-22 second promotional video for "Savdo-E" — a sales, POS, and inventory management platform for small and medium businesses across Central Asia (Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, Kyrgyzstan), serving 830,000+ entrepreneurs.

Brand colors: dark background #0E150F, primary neon green #2ECC71, secondary teal #1ABC9C. Style: modern, clean, "Cyber Organic" — premium tech aesthetic, bold sans-serif typography. All on-screen text in Uzbek language.

ACT 1 — INTRODUCTION (0:00–0:05, slow elegant animation):
Smooth fade/scale reveal of "Savdo-E" logo on dark background with soft green glow. Text fades in gracefully:
"Savdo-E" (large bold title)
"Savdo, POS va Ombor — bitta tizimda" (subtitle)
Calm, premium pacing, like a product reveal.

ACT 2 — WHAT IT DOES (0:05–0:14, faster pace, animated feature cards):
1. "Offline ishlaydi" — wifi-off icon morphing into checkmark (internet o'chsa ham sotuv to'xtamaydi)
2. "AI tahlil — Gemini" — brain/chart icon with insight popup "Qaysi mahsulot ko'p sotilmoqda?"
3. "Tezkor sotuv — 3 soniyada" — stopwatch + receipt animation
4. "5 til, 4 valyuta" — flags and currency symbols cycling (UZ, RU, EN, KZ, TJ, KG / UZS, KZT, TJS, KGS)
5. "Veb, Mobil va Admin Panel" — three connected device icons linking to one cloud node

ACT 3 — WHY IT MATTERS (0:14–0:20, confident punchy pacing):
"Daftar va Telegram guruhlardan — tartibli tizimga"
"Vaqtingizni tejaydi"
"Xatoliklarni kamaytiradi"
"830,000+ MDH tadbirkori uchun yaratilgan"

CLOSING CTA (0:20–0:23):
Savdo-E logo centered
"Bepul boshlang — 0 so'm/oy"
"Karta kerak emas"
Tagline: "Savdo boshqaruvi sodda qilindi."

Motion: smooth easing in Act 1, snappier energetic easing in Act 2-3. Mobile-first vertical format, trustworthy modern tech feel, not playful/cartoonish.
```

## 🔵 Runway / Pika uchun (qisqaroq, sahna-sahna alohida video kerak bo'lsa)

Bu platformalar uzun matnli promptni yaxshi tushunmasligi mumkin — har bir sahnani alohida generatsiya qilib, keyin CapCut'da birlashtirish tavsiya etiladi:

**Clip 1:** `Dark premium background, glowing green logo reveal animation, minimal tech aesthetic, slow cinematic zoom, 5 seconds`

**Clip 2:** `Animated UI cards popping up on dark background with neon green accents, fast tech-style motion graphics, feature icons, 9 seconds`

**Clip 3:** `Bold white and green text statements appearing with confident energetic motion on dark background, business growth theme, 6 seconds`

**Clip 4:** `Logo centered with call-to-action button glow effect, premium dark tech background, 3 seconds`
